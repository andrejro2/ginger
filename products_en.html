1. How to use time efficiently
You will learn how to identify your time wasting activities and how to manage your time. You will learn to identify your priorities, plan your day, or how to correctly delegate work tasks and how to say "no". 3. How to make smart decisions
You will learn how to change your perspective of problems and how to solve them successfully. In order to deal with problems, you need to be detached, learn to think outside of the box, to identify the essential elements, and to make decisions quickly. The course uses the fairy tale, "Three little pigs", as a scenario in business design :) so it is easy to remember the principles of problem solving. You will also find examples and practices that will inspire you in solving problematic situations.
